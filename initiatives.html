Our Initiatives

Committed to safety, awareness, and community partnership.

Community Policing

Building strong relationships with community members to collaboratively identify and solve safety issues.

Awareness Programs

Hosting workshops, seminars, and campaigns about crime prevention, traffic safety, and emergency preparedness.

Open Communication

Establishing open communication channels including hotlines and social media platforms to report issues and share updates.

Volunteer Support

Engaging dedicated volunteers to support community patrols, educational efforts, and victim assistance programs.

Technology Integration

Utilizing modern technology and data analytics to improve policing efficiency and responsiveness.

Emergency Alerts

Providing timely alerts and warnings to residents via WhatsApp, SMS, and social media channels.
