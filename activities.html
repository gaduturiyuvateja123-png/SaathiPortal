Activities & Events

Documenting our police community engagement through events and programs.

November 1, 2025
4:00 PM - 5:00 PM
The SKATHI app event conducted by CI T. Srinivasa Rao Garu at Dr. Besant School
At Dr. Besant School, CI Sri Vinas Rao Garu led a comprehensive awareness program about the SKATHI app focused on empowering school teachers and the community with knowledge about the app's safety features.
The session highlighted instant emergency response and SOS alerts features.
The program encouraged wider community awareness for safer environments.
