About SAATHI

Building Trust Through Connection

SAATHI is a pioneering initiative by 2 Town Police, Vizianagaram designed to bridge the gap between law enforcement and the community. Through this platform, we aim to foster transparency, cooperation, and mutual understanding.

Our mission is to create a safer community by working together with citizens, addressing concerns proactively, and building lasting relationships based on trust and respect.
